# XTool MetalFab Addons

Every xTool MetalFab owner will eventually run into these problems. This repository contains the solutions.
Open-source add-ons, fixtures and workflow improvements for the xTool MetalFab.

The goal is simple:
Make the machine more precise, more capable and easier to use.
Some projects are finished.
Most are prototypes.
A few are future problems already fixed.

---

## Smart Slats System

The standard zig-zag slats that come with the xTool MetalFab work well for sheet metal, but they are not ideal for many common fabrication tasks.
The Smart Slats System is a collection of interchangeable support slats designed for specific materials and workflows.

Examples include:
* Rectangular tube supports
* Round tube supports
* Precision end stops
* Pipe rotation fixtures
* Angle iron supports
* Deep-cut workholding solutions

All designs are intended to be practical, easy to manufacture and easy to modify.

## Current Projects
### Slat-01 Flat Rail
**Problem**
Rectangular tube stock rocks on the standard zig-zag slats.

**Solution**
Drop-in flat support rails that provide a stable and repeatable support surface.

**Status**
Planning

---

### Slat-02 Pipe Rail
**Problem**
Round tubes tend to roll and are difficult to position consistently.

**Solution**
Support rails with V-grooves for repeatable tube positioning.

**Status**
Concept

---

### Slat-03 End Stop Rail
**Problem**
Material is rarely positioned in exactly the same place twice.

**Solution**
A rigid positioning rail that creates a repeatable machine reference.

**Status**
Concept

---

### Slat-04 Pipe Rotator
**Problem**
Many tube operations require manual rotation between cuts.

**Solution**
A support and indexing system that allows quick and repeatable tube rotation.

**Status**
Concept

---

### Slat-05 Angle Iron Rail
**Problem**
L-profiles are difficult to support consistently on the standard bed.

**Solution**
Dedicated support geometry for angle iron and structural profiles.

**Status**
Concept

---

### Slat-06 Deep Cut Frame
**Problem**
Large profiles may exceed the vertical working envelope of the machine.

**Solution**
A lowered workholding system that increases usable cutting height.

**Status**
Concept
